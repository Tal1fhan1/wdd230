Content

Home page

Overview

The home page will have a basic overview of what Shonen anime is and where it came from. It will explain that Shonen anime is adapted from manga (Japanese comics) that have been released by a company called Shonen Jump

Target Audience

The main target audience of Shonen is teenagers. There are a lot of shows that feature a teenage protagonist or even an entire cast of teenagers. The lessons that can be learned are also usually geared to teenagers because they teach principles of self-worth, discipline and determination. These are all essential values to have during adolescent due to the fact that most problems that teenagers face are due to a lack of one or more of those values.

Genres and Themes

Most, if not all, of the shows that fall under the Shonen umbrella are heavily focused on fast paced action. That certainly isn't all that there is to it because they also contain elements of other genres sprinkled into them like Sci-fi and fantasy. They all have a wide range of themes that they tackle. Some shows focus on lighter themes like adventure and friendship. Other shows focus on darker themes such as the inevitability of death and futility of life.

Popularity

Why is it so popular?

Page 2 will focus on the specific elements found in Shonen Anime that has made it so popular and influential in pop culture.

Characters

There is no denying that some of the most popular and iconic characters in all of fiction are from Shonen Anime. Characters like Goku, Naruto and Luffy just to name a few who are the dictionary definition of iconic. Chances are, even if you don't recognise their names, you have definitely seen what they look like. Characters in these shows have been written in such a way that anyone can relate to and empathise with them. You get to see them succeed and fail, laugh and cry but most importantly you get to watch them grow and develop.

Well-crafted Worlds

The worlds that these shows take place in are all so unique and memorable. Some of them are worlds that we wish we could live in and some of them we would not want to step foot in. Either way they feel real because they have been built from the ground up to be believable. Even though they are not real, these worlds can sometimes feel more real than our own which is why people get really attached to them.

Influence

Why is it so influential?

Page 3 will focus on the influence it had on the world. Due to how Shonen anime has become, it has been very influential to pop culture and it started many tropes that are still followed to this day.

Iconic moves.

One word: Kamehameha. Even if you have not watched any Shonen anime, you know exactly what that is. That is the move that every fan of Dragon Ball has tried to do at least once in their life.

Transformations

There are a lot of shows that include characters that can transform. Some that immediately comes to mind are Ben 10 and Danny Phantom. As amazing as those shows are, they probably wouldn't even exist if it wasn't inspired by the transformations that were pioneered by shows like Dragon Ball Z. Everyone knows what it means to go 'Super Saiyan'. Sometimes it is even used in everyday conversation to describe someone who surpassed their limits. It is just that iconic.

Legendary quotes

There is a large library of incredible quotes in Shonen anime that are not only good life lessons but they are used frequently in memes other parts of pop culture.
